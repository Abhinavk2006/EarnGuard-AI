# 📌 EARNGUARD AI – Context-Aware Income Protection for Gig Workers

## 🚀 About the Project

**EARNGUARD AI** is an intelligent, context-aware income protection platform designed to safeguard gig workers—particularly urban delivery partners—from unpredictable income loss caused by external disruptions such as extreme weather, pollution, and sudden demand fluctuations. Traditional insurance systems rely on manual claims and static models, making them ineffective for dynamic gig-based work environments.

To address this, EarnGuard AI introduces a **parametric insurance model** that automatically detects income disruption scenarios and triggers payouts without requiring manual claim requests. The system continuously monitors environmental conditions, demand patterns, and worker activity to assess real-time risk.

At the core of the platform lies the **Income Loss Confidence Score (ILCS)**, which evaluates the likelihood of genuine income loss using environmental risk, demand drop, and worker activity. This enables fast, transparent, and fraud-resistant decision-making.

The platform integrates **machine learning models** to enhance intelligence:

* A **Linear Regression model** dynamically predicts insurance premiums
* A **Logistic Regression model** automates claim approval decisions

Additionally, the system includes a **financial management layer** with wallet-based payments, transaction tracking, and downloadable PDF bills and statements, creating a complete simulation of a real-world insurance and financial ecosystem.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Gig workers such as delivery partners face frequent income loss due to external disruptions like extreme weather, pollution, and sudden local restrictions. These events reduce working hours and directly impact their earnings. Existing insurance systems do not provide dynamic and automated income protection.
